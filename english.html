OUR HISTORY

With a deep and fixed look, from an apartment balcony across from the sea, in Santos city, São Paulo state, Brazil, place where we used to spend our summer vacation, the spanish immigrant Francisco Angelo Montolar Buil was sitting on a chair looking to the Atlantic ocean in the horizon. I, Francisco Angelo Montolar Buil Neto, his grandson, a ten years old boy, kept wondering in silence what kinds of things were passing through my grandfather's mind, that man that got off a ship very young in that same port, with his family, and with a lot of hard work, persistence and dedicaton managed to build his life in Brazil, a country that sheltered them for living. When my grandparents arrived in Brazil with their family, they started working in a coffee farming in Jaú city, São Paulo State and later, after working very hard, they established themselves as traders. He passed by several cities, and at the beginning of 1930, he settled down in Marília city, São Paulo state, my grandfather now married to Dirna Montolar, also a spanish immigrant that was starting a colonization. Still as a trader, he bought his first farm with coffee crops and also built a coffee processing and trading company, buying and selling coffee from farmers around him. In 1942, together with his brother Gregorio Montolar Buil, he bought a clod of earth in Sagres town, São Paulo and transformed it in a fertile farm. Later, the founder Francisco Angelo Montolar Buil died and, at the beginning of 1970, the farm started to be ruled by Manoel Montolar Pelesel, the founder's son "my father", that modernized and prepared it for the future. After the 1990 decade I started to rule the farm with my father, where I live with my family until today. This history that I summarize guided me through all my life. In 1993 I founded a trading representation company that worked only in Brazil, and after years this company today named "Gestão & Negócios Trade comércio de exportação e importação" started working abroad. Our company aims to prospect for products and goods for potential customers abroad, and promote the trade interchange between nations. Finding target products according to our customers needs is our biggest motivation. Transparency, trust and longevity in our business is our gratest aim.That deep look that my grandfather used to give to the ocean always motivated me to find out the wonders beyond that horizon. With this porpose, I've been led to know many countries and cultures.
